Help & Guide

Welcome to ArcheryKeeper! 🎯

This app is designed to make keeping your archery scores as easy as tapping a button. Here is your quick-start guide.

Good to Know: How Your Score is Saved

You do not need to manually hit a "save" button. ArcheryKeeper runs right in your browser and automatically saves every single tap directly to your phone's memory. If you accidentally close your browser, switch to a different app, or lose cell service, don't panic! Just open the page back up, and you will be dropped right back into your round exactly where you left off.

Starting Target (Shotgun Start)

If you are shooting a 3D round, you can specify exactly which target you are starting at on the course. The app will adapt the numbering for you. If you realize you entered the wrong starting target midway through the shoot, just tap the Target Number at the top of the scoring screen to fix it.

Correct Starting Target

This will correctly shift your existing scores to match the new starting target number.
